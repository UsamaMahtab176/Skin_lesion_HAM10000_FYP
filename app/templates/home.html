Welcome to SkinAI

SkinAI is a cutting-edge AI-powered skin lesions diagnosis tool designed to assist in dermatology. Our advanced model can accurately identify and provide detailed information about various skin conditions.

SkinAI leverages state-of-the-art artificial intelligence and deep learning technologies to analyze extreme skin conditions or lesions like:

  • Actinic keratosis
  • Basal cell carcinoma (bcc)
  • Benign keratosis like lesions (bkl)
  • Dermatofibroma (df)
  • Melanoma (mel)
  • Melanocytic nevi (nv)"
  • Vascular lesions (vasc)

Our platform is designed to assist dermatologists and healthcare professionals in their diagnosis and provide patients with valuable information about their skin health.
